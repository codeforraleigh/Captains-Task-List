NC Open Pass

Your 2017 pass to the premier open source, open government, open data, and other innovative events around the Triangle. You pay once and you're ready to attend any of the NC Open Pass affiliated events throughout the year. Start your ideas at Civic Spark Day, nuture your projects and build your teams at CityCamp NC, and conclude your open data project at NC DataPalooza.

Early bird passes are $40 through September 4, 2017 and $50 starting on September 5, 2017.

Events include

August 2017

Civic Spark Day

Civic Spark Day - New for 2017, brainstorm creative solutions to community problems and begin your public innovation journey on August 12, 2017.

September 2017

CityCamp NC

CityCamp NC - is an annual event bringing citizens, government, and businesses together to openly innovate and improve our quality of life through technology on September 28-30, 2017. CityCamp NC includes the NC DataPalooza open data hackathon.

October 2017

All Things Open

All Things Open - Pass holders get access to free pitch events for NC DataPalooza and discounted rates for the conference held at the Raleigh Convention Center on October 23-24 2017.

November 2017

NC DataPalooza

NC DataPalooza - An open data competition to catalyze positive community and economic impact with coaching and cash prizes on November 13, 2017.

About

NC Open Pass is a partnership between Code for Cary, Code for Durham, Code for Raleigh, and NC DataPalooza—making our innovative events in the Triangle available together on one ticket.
