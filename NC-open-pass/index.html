NC Open Pass

Your 2016 pass to four open source, open government, open data, and other innovative events around the Triangle. You pay once and you're ready to attend any of the NC Open Pass affiliated events throughout the year.

Early bird passes are $40 through March 31, 2016 and $50 starting on April 1, 2016.
